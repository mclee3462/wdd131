PC Building Site Plan

Purpose

This website will guide users through the process of building a PC, from choosing components to assembly and troubleshooting. It will serve as a resource for both beginners and experienced builders.

Audience

The primary audience includes gaming enthusiasts, content creators, and tech-savvy individuals looking to build their own PCs.

Dynamic Elements

The site will include an interactive PC parts picker, component compatibility checker, and a FAQ section with collapsible answers.

Logo

A custom logo representing PC hardware and assembly will be designed using Canva.

Colors and Fonts

Colors: Dark blue, gray, and neon green for a techy look.

Fonts: Roboto and Montserrat for readability and a modern feel.

Content

Pages will include guides, step-by-step instructions, recommended PC builds, and troubleshooting tips.

Wireframes

Images of wireframes for homepage and build guide page will be included.
